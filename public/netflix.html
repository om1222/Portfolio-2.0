The Problem

No unified view of a global catalog

🌍
Inconsistent country names
"USA", "U.S.A.", "United States" — 3 variants, 1 country. Distorting geo-insights by 35%
🏷️
Multi-value genre fields
Genres as comma-separated strings. Impossible to GROUP BY or aggregate accurately in SQL
📊
No KPI visibility
Zero consolidated view on catalog size, genre mix, release trends, or content gaps across regions
⚡
Slow dashboard loads
No pre-aggregated tables or indexes — Power BI running on raw joins, every visual slow to respond
