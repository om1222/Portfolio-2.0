Cancellation Fix
Wrong Address = #1 customer cancellation (2,362 cases). A simple address-validation prompt at booking eliminates ~22% of all customer cancellations with minimal engineering effort.
Revenue Optimization
UPI users cancel less and complete more rides. Targeted UPI cashback during peak hours (6–8PM) can convert borderline fare hesitations and lift avg. ride revenue by ~4%.
Supply Planning
Evening rush at 6PM peaks at 12,397 rides/hour — 9× the late-night baseline. Pre-allocating driver supply to 6–8PM windows can dramatically cut "No Driver Found" failures.
Automation Impact
Tableau dashboard with LOD expressions replaced 10+ hours/month of manual Excel KPI work for ops teams — enabling real-time decisions instead of week-old data.
