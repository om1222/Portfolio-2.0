Inventory Strategy
Low Fat SKUs should be stocked at 2× levels. 65% of $1.2M revenue comes from this category — any stockout in Low Fat items directly impacts the majority of sales.
Location Strategy
Tier 3 outlets generate 38.3% of revenue — highest of all tiers. Counter-intuitive to urban-first assumptions. Tier 3 expansion has measurable positive ROI that this EDA surfaces.
Outlet Performance
Outlets established in 1985 and 1999 show peak sales — mature, high-trust locations. Newer outlets (2004–2009) show a clear upward trend, validating Blinkit's expansion strategy.
Marketing Signal
Fruits & Vegetables leads all 16 categories at $178K. Top 3 categories contribute 37% of total revenue — clear priority for supply chain focus and promotional campaigns.
