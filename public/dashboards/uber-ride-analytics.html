01 — Cancellation
25% cancellation rate across 150K rides. Driver cancellations (18%) outnumber customer ones (7%). Primary driver causes: customer-related issues & health concerns — operational interventions can reduce this by 5–7%.
02 — Revenue & UPI
UPI dominates at 40% of total revenue (₹2.07Cr). Cash is 2nd at 25.0%. Targeted UPI cashback promotions can drive higher digital adoption and project a ~4% revenue uplift through increased ride completion rates.
03 — Peak Hours
Evening rush (6PM) peaks at 12,397 rides/hour — 9× the late-night baseline. Morning rush hits 8–10AM. Driver allocation should be front-loaded to these windows to reduce No Driver Found (7%) failures.
04 — Wrong Address
Wrong Address is the #1 customer cancellation reason (2,362 cases). Address validation checks at booking — a low-engineering-effort fix — can eliminate a significant share of preventable customer cancellations.
05 — Auto Dominates
Auto contributes the most revenue at ₹1.29Cr due to sheer ride volume (25,415 rides). Premier Sedan has the highest avg revenue per ride. Short Auto rides during off-peak have the lowest ROI and highest cancellation risk.
06 — Automation Impact
Tableau dashboard automated all 6 KPIs replacing manual Excel exports. Estimated 10+ hours/month saved for operations teams, with live drill-down filtering by vehicle type, time, zone, and payment mode.
